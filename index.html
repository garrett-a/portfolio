About me

My name is Garrett Aldridge and I love clearing the way for beautiful designs to come to life. With a passion for problem-solving and a keen eye for details, I am able to help develop websites that best fit a client's needs. In 2020, after ten years as a chef, I wrote my first line of code and quickly found myself desiring to learn more. After fully immersing myself in the world of development it was clear that I had a new passion to bring designs to life.

I am just as passionate about development as I am about my hobbies of rock climbing, video games, and cooking. I want to learn every aspect of the craft and will not stop until things are perfect.
